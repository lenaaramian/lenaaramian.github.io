Financial Markets and Instruments (FNCE90047)

I was the course coordinator for Financial Markets and Instruments (FNCE90047) in the Department of Finance at the University of Melbourne. This subject introduces domestic and international financial markets, key trading instruments, and financial institutions. Topics covered include financial system structure, money and capital market instruments, financial market dynamics such as volatility and risk, interest rates, inflation, securities regulation, taxation in finance, and various market developments including futures, options, and swaps.
