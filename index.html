About me

I am a researcher in finance at Stockholm Business School, Stockholm University, Sweden. Prior to this, I worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Finance at the University of Melbourne, Australia. I received my Ph.D. in Finance and MSc. in Mathematical Statistics from Stockholm University. My research in market microstructure focuses on market design and financial market regulation. I analyze the impact of financial market design and regulatory reforms on the competition for order flow between different trading systems, traders' order routing strategies, and the subsequent effects on the market and execution quality.
